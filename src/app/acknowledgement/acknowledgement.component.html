Dependency on Google Play Services

Currently, OmniTrack for Android is heavily depends on Google Play Services, including GCM and Firebase. This implies that OmniTrack cannot be targeted for the citizens in the countries which are restricting the access to the Google services (China is one prominent region for such restrictions).
